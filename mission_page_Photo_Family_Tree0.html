Photo Family Tree
Creating and Decorating a Family Tree with Gathered Family Members' Photos
Guidelines
Materials : Family photos, colored pencils, large paper, stickers (optional) etc.
 Time required: Approximately 2 hours.

 Program Objectives:
 1. Family History Tracking
2. Preserving Family Memories
3. Strengthening Family Bonds

 Program Effects:
 1. Enhancement of Family Unity
2. Recording Family History
3. Strengthening Emotional Connections within the Family
