Stars in the Night
This program aims to bring family members together to discover constellations, share interests, and strengthen family bonds through an enjoyable activity of stargazing.
Materials : Flashlight or portable telescope (optional), constellation map, mobile device, pens, large paper, optional constellation observation journal, outdoor blanket or chairs.
 Time : Approximately 1.5 to 2 hours.

 Objectives
 1. Strengthen cooperation and teamwork.
2. Enhance family bonds.
3. Stimulate curiosity about nature and the universe.

 Effects
 1. Improve communication and cooperation skills.
2. Enhance family cohesion through shared experiences.
3. Broaden understanding and interest in nature and the universe.
