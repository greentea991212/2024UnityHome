Love Letter
Materials: Writing utensils, stationery, open hearts.
Time required: Approximately 10 minutes.

Program Objectives:
1. Strengthen emotional bonds among family members.
2. Facilitate communication.
3. Express gratitude.

Program Effects:
1. Enhance relationships among family members.
2. Improve emotional stability.
3. Foster self-awareness and personal growth.
A program where parents write love letters to their children and children write love letters to their parents.
